# 🎬 Netflix Content Analysis

A comprehensive Data Analysis project exploring Netflix's global content catalog using Python, Pandas, NumPy, Statistics, and Plotly.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Netflix is one of the world's largest streaming platforms with thousands of Movies and TV Shows available across different countries and genres.

The goal of this project is to analyze Netflix's content strategy, identify growth trends, understand content distribution, and generate business insights using data analytics techniques.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Understand Netflix's content composition.
* Analyze Movies vs TV Shows distribution.
* Identify top content-producing countries.
* Study Netflix's content growth over time.
* Analyze ratings and audience targeting.
* Discover dominant genres and directors.
* Generate actionable business insights.

## 🔍 Key Findings

### 🎬 Content Distribution

* Movies dominate Netflix's catalog.
* Approximately 70% of the platform's content consists of Movies.

### 🌍 Global Expansion

* Netflix content is available from hundreds of country combinations.
* The United States contributes the highest number of titles.
* India ranks among the top content-producing markets.

### 📈 Rapid Growth

* Significant content growth occurred after 2016.
* Netflix aggressively expanded its catalog between 2017 and 2020.

### 🎭 Genre Insights

* Drama-based content dominates the platform.
* International Movies represent a major portion of Netflix's catalog.

### 🔞 Audience Focus

* TV-MA is the most common rating category.
* Netflix primarily targets mature audiences.

## 💼 Business Recommendations

* Continue expanding investments in international content.
* Increase production of high-performing genres.
* Balance the catalog with more original TV Shows.
